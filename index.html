Model & Task
Task
3-Class Classification
Predicts patient outcome from clinical notes: Positive (treatment success), Negative (deterioration), Uncertain (monitoring required).
Model
distilbert-base-uncased
Fine-tuned on clinical text with max_length=256. 3 epochs, batch size 16. Custom classifier head with dropout for regularisation.
Clinical Value
Automated triage of clinical notes to flag negative outcomes for immediate review. Reduces manual screening time in high-volume clinical settings.
Ethical Considerations & Limitations
What this model should NOT do
Make autonomous clinical decisions. Replace physician judgment. Be used without human oversight. Be deployed on patient populations different from its training distribution.
Bias considerations
Training data may not represent all patient demographics equally. Model performance should be evaluated separately across age groups, sexes, and ethnicities before clinical deployment.
Uncertainty quantification
Softmax probabilities are not well-calibrated confidence scores. Bayesian fine-tuning or temperature scaling is recommended before clinical use to provide reliable uncertainty estimates.
Regulatory context
Clinical AI tools are subject to MHRA (UK), FDA (US), and CE marking (EU) requirements. This project is a research prototype and not a medical device.
